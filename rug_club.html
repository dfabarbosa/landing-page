About the program
The Rug Club is a place for the Ruggable community to come together and get rewarded for providing feedback and honest opinions. Our goal is to serve you!
Yes, our brand is focused on rugs...but we’re interested in the home. Your home. How you style it, what you’re inspired by, and what you want to see next. This isn’t just another email newsletter, it’s a two way conversation around your passions and ideas for home decor so we can best serve our community.
